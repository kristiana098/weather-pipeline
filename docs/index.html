Weather Poem

**English:**

Aalborg, a chill to greet,
10.4 degrees, the wind to meet.
Copenhagen's calm, a gentle breeze,
11.5 degrees, a peaceful tease.

Athens, a warm and sunny place,
22.1 degrees, a smiling face.
The world is full of contrasts, so bright,
Where would you choose, for a lovely night?

Tomorrow, I would choose to be,
In Athens, where the sun shines free.
The warm breeze whispers sweet delight,
A perfect day, in the Greek light.

**Danmarksk ( Danish )**

Aalborg en kulde der venter,
10,4 grader, vinden i vejen.
København, en rolig dag,
11,5 grader, et spild af tid.

Athens, et varmt og solskinsted,
22,1 grader, et smil på dybden.
Verden er fyldt med modsætninger,
Hvor ville du være, i morgenrøden?

I morgen ville jeg være,
i Athens, hvor solen skiner fri.
Vinden hvisler, i ly lykkelig,
Den perfekte dag, i græsk lys.

Note: 
This poem is just a comparison between the three places, and a poetic way to decide which city might be nicer to visit tomorrow.
