Weather Poem

**English Version**

Like three sisters, side by side, 
Aalborg, Athens, Copenhagen reside. 
Their weather whispers tales of difference and might,
A dance of wind and sun, a symphony so bright.

Aalborg's chill, 13°C, a gentle breeze to bear,
A soothing caress on skin so fair.
Copenhagen's gust, 24.1 km/h, a wild and raging storm,
A force to reckoned with, a challenge to form.

Athens, the gem, 23.3°C, a warm and golden glow,
A haven of tranquility, a place to grow.
No rain for any of them, a blessing so true,
A day for adventure, a day for you.

Tomorrow, I'd choose Athens' gentle heat,
To stroll along its streets, to feel its warmth so sweet.
For when the sun shines bright, and the breeze is light,
Athens is the place, where all is right.

**Danskt Version**

Som tre søstre, side om side, 
Aalborg, Athen, København residér. 
Vejrfrisen hvisler fortællinger om forskellighed og magt,
En danse af vind og sol, en symfoni så lys.

Aalborgs kulde, 13°C, en blid brise at bære,
En trøstende berøring på hud så fager.
Københavns gale vind, 24.1 km/h, en vilde og stormende storm,
En kraft til at regne med, en udfordring til at forme.

Athen, perlen, 23,3°C, en varm og gylden glede,
Et paradis af ro, et sted til at vokse.
Ingen regn for nogen af dem, en gave så sand,
En dag til eventyr, en dag for dig.

I morgen, jeg ville vælge Athen's varme væld,
At vandre gennem dens gader, at føle dens varme så dejlige.
For når solen skiner lys, og vinden er let,
Athen er det sted, hvor alt er i orden.

Note: This is a short poetic comparison, and it's not a scientifically accurate analysis of the weather.
