Weather Poem

**English Version:**

Whispers of weather weave a tale,
Of three cities, each with its own gale.
Aalborg's gentle breeze, a soothing sigh,
14.6°C, a calm and peaceful sky.

Athens' warmth, a fiery blaze, 
25.9°C, where sunbeams dance and play.
But as we compare, the winds do tell,
Aalborg's stillness, a peaceful spell to dwell.

Copenhagen's cool, a moderate hue,
14.2°C, a balanced air to woo.
Yet, as the sun sets, and day succumbs to night,
Aalborg's warmth, a gentle delight.

Tomorrow's forecast, a question to ask,
But for now, Aalborg's calm, a shelter to ask.

**Danske Version:**

Veernes sprog hvisler en historie,
Om tre byer, hver med sit eget vindes vugge.
Aalborgs blide brise, en hvæsende suslen,
14,6°C, et roligt og fredeligt himmelblik.

Athens varme, en ildflamme,
25,9°C, hvor solens stråler danser og leger.
Men når vi sammenligner, vindene fortæller,
Aalborgs stillehed, en fredelig bøn til hus.

Københavns kulde, en moderat nuance,
14,2°C, en balance og kølig luft til trøste.
Men da solen sænker sig og dagtabet nærmer sig nat,
Aalborgs varme, en behagelig tilbederi.

Morgendagens vædrende, en spørgsmål til spørge,
Men hvad nu om dages, Aalborgs rolige, en beskærmet hus.

I think of a more poetic way.

Aalborg's breeze whispers 'come and stay',
while Athens' warmth beckons all day.
Copenhagen's coolness dances in between,
but Aalborg's calm wins the weather scene.

Tomorrow, Aalborg's the place to be,
for a peaceful day, calm as can be.
Leave the heat and the cold to roam,
Aalborg's gentle weather is home.
