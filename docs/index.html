Weather Poem

**In Perfect Harmony: A Weather Symphony**

Across the lands we find,
Three cities, diverse in design.

Aalborg's gentle breeze whispers low,
12.5°C, a warmth to know.
No rain falls, just a clear blue sky,
A perfect day, as the wind glides by.

Athens, a city of ancient might,
14.5°C, but rain in sight.
44.7mm falls, a deluge strong,
A somber mood, where music is wrong.

Copenhagen's calm, a soothing hush,
12.9°C, a peaceful rush.
No rain beats down, just a gentle sway,
A haven from the world's disarray.

**Hvor er det aller fineste om morgen?** (Where is it the most beautiful tomorrow?)

Copenhagen! A day of serene delight,
A place to bask in morning's golden light.
Leave Aalborg and Athens aside,
And find your peace in Copenhagen's gentle tide.

(Translation of above poem in Danish)

**I perfekt harmoni: en vejrets symfoni**

Over jorden finder vi,
Tre byer, forskellige i design.

Aalborgs milde bævrende hvisler lavt,
12,5°C, en varme at kende.
Ingenting falder, blot en klar blå himmel,
Et perfekt dag, hvor vinden glider forbi.

Athens, en by af gamle storhed,
14,5°C, men regn i sight.
44,7 mm falder, et stærkt tilbageslag,
En sørgmodig stemning, hvor musikken går galt.

København, et stille kalde, en rolig bølge,
12,9°C, en fredelig bølge.
Ingenting banker ned, blot en mild bølgelignende,
Et tilflugtssted fra verdens kaos.

**Hvor er det allerfineste om morgen?**

København! En dag af roligt glæde,
Et sted til at bade i morgens guld.
Lå Aalborg og Athen til side,
Og find fred i København's rolige bølge.
