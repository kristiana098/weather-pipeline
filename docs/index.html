Weather Poem

Weather's whispers, soft and low,
Echoing tales of lands we know.

Aalborg's chill, a biting sigh,
8.7 degrees, a sombre sky,
Rain falls gentle, a melancholy hush,
34.9 km/h, a wind that rushes.

Athens gleams, a radiant smile,
17.9 degrees, a warm and gentle while,
A mere 0.3mm, a fleeting dew,
10.5 km/h, a breeze that whispers anew.

Copenhagen's heart, a tranquil sea,
10.2 degrees, a soothing melody,
1.1 mm, a kiss of rain so fine,
31.3 km/h, a wind that whispers in rhyme.

Tomorrow's tale, a day to choose,
Perhaps in Athens, where sunbeams muse,
The warm breeze whispers, a gentle caress,
17.9 degrees, a haven of bliss.

Dagets løb, hvilken stad vil være bedst i dag?
Kun i Athen, hvor solens stråler viser vej.

(In Danish)
Dagets løb, hvilken stad vil være bedst i dag?
Kun i Athen, hvor solens stråler viser vej.

(Translation)
The day's run, which city will be the best today?
Only in Athens, where the sun's rays show the way.
