Weather Poem

**Weather's Ephemeral Dance**

Two cities bask in warmth beside the sea,
Athens' sun-kissed days, a haven for thee.
Twenty-two degrees, a radiant delight,
A gentle breeze whispers through the Athenian night.

In distant Europe, Aalborg's chill does lay,
Nine-point-nine degrees, a cooler day.
Scanty rain, a whisper of moisture's kiss,
A brisk wind stirs, the city's autumnist wish.

Copenhagen's heart, a balance 'twixt the three,
A mild day dawns, with a wind that blows free.
Ten-point-eight degrees, a gentle reprieve,
A fleeting calm, in winter's ebb and flow, to leave.

**Hvor er det bedste sted at være i morgen?**

Morgen vil Athen være det bedste sted at være,
med sin solbeskinnede dage og milde, varme luft.
Tjek vejret i Athen før du planlægger din dag!

(Translation: "Where is the best place to be tomorrow?
Tomorrow Athens will be the best place to be,
with its sun-kissed days and mild, warm air.
Check the weather in Athens before you plan your day!")
