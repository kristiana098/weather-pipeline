Weather Poem

**English Version**

Three cities, three different skies,
Aalborg, Athens, Copenhagen's sighs.

Aalborg's chill begins the tale,
Rain-kissed and windswept, with a gentle gale.
But Athens shines bright with radiant zest,
Warmer hearts, but rain's gentle caress.
Copenhagen's calm, a soothing sight,
Rain-free haven, on this cloudy night.

Tomorrow, if I may choose to roam,
I'd flee to Athens, where sunshine's home.

**Dansk Version**

Tre byer, tre forskellige himleregaler
Aalborg, Athen, Københavns susen

Aalborgs kærlighed til kølig begynder
Regnpledd og vinddragende, med en blid ståls.
Men Athen lyser op med varm og lys strå
Varmere hjerter, men regnets blide blus.
København er rolig, en behagelig syn
Regnfri have, på disse tåge nätter.

I morgen, hvis jeg får lov at vandre
Jeg vælger Athen, hvor solslys hjem.

Note: The poetic comparison aims to evoke a sense of atmosphere and mood for each city, rather than being a literal, factual description of the weather. The choice of which city to visit on a particular day is subjective, and in this poem, I suggest Athens as the nicest choice on a hypothetical day. However, this is meant to be a whimsical and creative exercise rather than a serious recommendation based on actual weather patterns.
