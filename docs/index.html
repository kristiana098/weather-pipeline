Weather Poem

**English Version**

A gentle breeze caresses Aalborg's shore,
Where autumn's warmth brings 13 degrees once more.
Rain and wind depart, in a peaceful sigh,
Leaving the city calm, beneath a cloudless sky.

In contrast, Athens basks beneath the radiant sun,
25 degrees await, its summer fun begun.
Yet, whispers of a drizzle hint at rain,
Reminding the city's residents of impending pain.

Copenhagen holds a steady hand, 14 degrees it keeps,
Winds whispers soft, as autumn's leaves gently creep.
Here, harmony is found, in weather's gentle sway,
For those who prefer the comfort of a mid-spring day.

**Where to be tomorrow?**
If I were to choose,
I'd stroll through Copenhagen's streets, with autumn's hue,
Beneath the calm of gentle winds, and skies so blue.

**Denmark Version**

**Et kærlig bånd streifer Aalborgs kyst,**
Hvor efterårets varme bringer 13 grader,
 Regn og vind forlader, i en rolig suge,
Ladende byen rolig, under en klar himmel.

I modsætning, Athen bager under det strålende sol,
25 grader venter, dens sommer-fest begynder.
Men hvisken om et drille hint om regn,
Hindre byens beboere på kommande smerte.

København holder en tryg hånd, 14 grader det behold,
Vindens hvisken blødt, som efterårets blade blomstrer.
Her finder harmoni sted, i vejr's bløde svævning,
For de som foretrækker komforten af en sommersol.

**Hvor ville jeg gerne være i morgen?**
Hvis jeg må vælge,
Så ville jeg vandre gennem København's gader, med efterårets glans,
Beneath roen af bløde vind og himmel blå.

Note: The poetic comparisons between the cities are made based on a subjective interpretation of the weather data provided.
