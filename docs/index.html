Weather Poem

**A poetic comparison**

The winds they whisper secrets cold,
In Aalborg's grey, the storm does unfold.
Raindrops fall like tears from above,
A chill of 8.6°C, a sorrow to love.

But farther south, the sun begins to shine,
In Athens' warmth, the heart can entwine.
Sixteen degrees, a gentle breeze so light,
A city alive, where beauty takes flight.

Copenhagen's streets, a mix of cold and grey,
A city's heartbeat, in a melancholy sway.
Ten degrees, a gentle rain's caress,
A city's charm, in a quiet, introspective dress.

**Hvor er det dejligt at være i morgen?**

Det er Athen, hvor det varmt og solskinsagtigt er. Her er det 16.3°C, svært lidt regn og en blundred bise. En perfekt dag til at nyde byens skønhed og varme.

**Where is it nice to be tomorrow?**

It's Athens, where it's warm and sunny. Here it's 16.3°C, very little rain and a gentle breeze. A perfect day to enjoy the city's beauty and warmth.
