Weather Poem

**Poetic Comparison**

Three cities, three skies, a tale of contrast so fine,
From north to south, a weather dance divine.

Aalborg, where the wind whispers low,
A gentle 16.7°C, a spring breeze to know.
The sun shines bright, with nary a cloud in sight,
A perfect day, a gentle, summer light.

Athens, a haven, where warmth and joy reside,
23.9°C, a sun-kissed paradise, where lovers abide.
The gentle breeze, a caress to the skin,
A place to bask, where sun-kissed memories begin.

Copenhagen, a chilly friend, a spring's reluctant sigh,
15.3°C, a breeze that whispers, "winter's not far from by".
The wind whispers secrets, of the changing seasons' sway,
A city waking, from a winter's gray.

**Where is it nicest to be tomorrow?**

English:
Tomorrow, it is clear that Athens shines bright and fair,
A haven of warmth, where sun-kissed memories share.
So pack your bags, and head to Greece's sunny land,
For a day of joy, in this Athenian paradise so grand.

Danish:
I morgen er det tydeligt, at Aten lyser klar og skønt,
Ett paradis af varme, hvor solen lyser over alt.
Så pak dine tasker, og rej om til Grækenlands solfyldte land,
For en dag af gladhed i dette Atheniske paradis, så dejligt og vidunderligt!
