Weather Poem

**Weather Wonders**

In Aalborg's misty veil, 
the wind whispers secrets, 
13.6°C, where raindrops fall, 
a gentle melody to enthrall.

In Athens' warm, golden light, 
22.7°C, a radiant sight, 
the sun shines bright, with gentle breeze, 
a haven of warmth and ease.

In Copenhagen's crisp, cool air, 
11.4°C, with winds that blow with care, 
a city of charm, with life so free, 
a winter wonderland for you and me.

Tomorrow, in my heart, I stay 
in Athens, where love and sun will sway, 
bathing in its warmth, with heart so light, 
 Athens wins the day.

Ånd for i morgen
vælger jeg Athen, 
det varme gryning 
giver mig glæde.

Men hvis du synes den kølige vind 
i København kan bære mig væk, 
eller hvis Aalborgs månstråle 
fører dig mod en mystisk vej, 
så vælg den vej, der føler rettig.

(Translation: But if you think the cool wind in Copenhagen can carry you away, or if Aalborg's moonlight leads you to a mystical way, then choose the path that feels right for you).
