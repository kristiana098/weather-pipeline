Weather Poem

**Poetic Comparison**

Three cities, three skies so fine,
Aalborg's chill, a gentle breeze divine.
13.5°C, a gentle caress so true,
A wind that whispers secrets, just for you.

But look to Athens, where sunbeams play,
21.0°C, a warm and vibrant day,
The Aegean breeze, a gentle lover's kiss,
The cradle of civilizations, where history persists.

And in Copenhagen, a Nordic charm,
10.4°C, a crisp and refreshing form,
A wind that stirs the senses, with a lively sway,
The city of cyclists, where freedom holds its way.

**Where to be tomorrow...?**

Hvis man skal vælge et sted til morgen:
Athens er den bedste valg. 
21.0°C og sol, giver en dejlig dag, der vil gøre dine fornøjet!

Translation: If you have to choose a place for tomorrow: Athens is the best choice. 21.0°C and sunshine will make for a wonderful day that will satisfy your desires.

English: If you have to choose a place for tomorrow, Athens is the best choice. 21.0°C and sunshine will make for a wonderful day that will satisfy your desires.
